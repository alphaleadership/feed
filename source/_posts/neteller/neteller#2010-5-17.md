# Neteller - Fuite de données

**Date de la fuite** : 2010-05-17  
**Nombre de comptes affectés** : 3 619 948

## Description
In May 2010, the e-wallet service known as Neteller suffered a data breach which exposed over 3.6M customers. The breach was not discovered until October 2015 and included names, email addresses, home addresses and account balances.

## Données exposées
- Account balances
- Dates of birth
- Email addresses
- Genders
- IP addresses
- Names
- Phone numbers
- Physical addresses
- Security questions and answers
- Website activity
