# BTC-E - Fuite de données

**Date de la fuite** : 2014-10-01  
**Nombre de comptes affectés** : 568 340

## Description
In October 2014, the Bitcoin exchange BTC-E was hacked and 568k accounts were exposed. The data included email and IP addresses, wallet balances and hashed passwords.

## Données exposées
- Account balances
- Email addresses
- IP addresses
- Passwords
- Usernames
- Website activity
