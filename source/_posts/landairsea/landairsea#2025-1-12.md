# LandAirSea - Fuite de données

**Date de la fuite** : 2025-01-12  
**Nombre de comptes affectés** : 337 373

## Description
In January 2025, the GPS tracking service LandAirSea suffered a data breach that exposed 337k unique customer email addresses alongside names, usernames and password hashes. The breach also exposed partial credit card data card type, last 4 digits and expiration, and GPS device identifiers and locations. LandAirSea is aware of the breach and has remediated the underlying vulnerability.

## Données exposées
- Email addresses
- Names
- Partial credit card data
- Passwords
- Physical addresses
- Usernames
