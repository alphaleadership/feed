# Stalker Online - Fuite de données

**Date de la fuite** : 2020-05-05  
**Nombre de comptes affectés** : 1 385 472

## Description
In May 2020, over 1.3M records from the MMO game Stalker Online were breached. The data included email and IP addresses, usernames and hashed passwords.

## Données exposées
- Email addresses
- IP addresses
- Passwords
- Usernames
