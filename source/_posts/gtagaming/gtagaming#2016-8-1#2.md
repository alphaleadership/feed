# GTAGaming - Fuite de données

**Date de la fuite** : 2016-08-01  
**Nombre de comptes affectés** : 197 184

## Description
In August 2016, the Grand Theft Auto forum GTAGaming was hacked and nearly 200k user accounts were leaked. The vBulletin based forum included usernames, email addresses and password hashes.

## Données exposées
- Dates of birth
- Email addresses
- IP addresses
- Passwords
- Usernames
- Website activity
