# Young Living Essential Oils - Fuite de données

**Date de la fuite** : 2024-12-11  
**Nombre de comptes affectés** : 1 128 951

## Description
In December 2024, data claimed to be breached from the multi-level marketing company Young Living Essential Oils was posted to a popular hacking forum. The data contained 1.1M unique email addresses alongside names, the country of the account and in many cases, their date of birth. Young Living Essential Oils did not respond to multiple attempts to contact them about the data.

## Données exposées
- Dates of birth
- Email addresses
- Geographic locations
- Names
